Hi!

I'm Alison, the creator behind the Wholesome Home Cook. A community focused on wholesome food and learning basic cooking techniques.

My mission is to help make cooking more fun, make you feel more confident in the kitchen, and connect like minded people together through this community.

I have been cooking for over 16 years starting out in a 4 year culinary program and continued cooking almost everyday from there. Now, I get to help others learn how to cook wholesome food at home!
